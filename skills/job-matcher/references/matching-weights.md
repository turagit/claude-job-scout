# Matching Weight Customization

## Default Weights

| Dimension | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Skills Match | 30% |
| Experience Alignment | 25% |
| Requirements Fit | 25% |
| Growth & Culture | 10% |
| Practical Factors | 10% |

## Career Stage Adjustments

### Entry Level (0-2 years)

- Skills Match: 20% (fewer skills expected)
- Experience Alignment: 15% (less experience to match)
- Requirements Fit: 25%
- Growth & Culture: 25% (growth opportunity matters most)
- Practical Factors: 15%

### Mid-Level (3-7 years)

- Use default weights

### Senior Level (8-15 years)

- Skills Match: 25%
- Experience Alignment: 30% (domain expertise critical)
- Requirements Fit: 25%
- Growth & Culture: 10%
- Practical Factors: 10%

### Executive (15+ years)

- Skills Match: 15% (leadership over technical)
- Experience Alignment: 35% (industry and scale of experience)
- Requirements Fit: 25%
- Growth & Culture: 15% (culture fit at executive level is critical)
- Practical Factors: 10%

### Freelance / Contract (any level)

- Skills Match: 35% (can I do this job right now?)
- Experience Alignment: 20% (capability over tenure)
- Requirements Fit: 30% (rate, remote, duration, IR35 are critical)
- Growth & Culture: 5% (short-term engagement, less relevant)
- Practical Factors: 10%

## Industry Adjustments

### Tech / Startups

- Increase Skills Match by 5% (specific tech stack matters)
- Decrease Experience Alignment by 5% (startups value capability over tenure)

### Finance / Consulting

- Increase Experience Alignment by 5% (pedigree and tenure matter)
- Decrease Growth & Culture by 5%

### Government / Public Sector

- Increase Requirements Fit by 10% (strict qualification requirements)
- Decrease Growth & Culture by 10%

## Scoring Thresholds

### Strict Mode (competitive markets)

- A-Tier: 90-100
- B-Tier: 75-89
- C-Tier: 60-74
- D-Tier: Below 60

### Relaxed Mode (broader search)

- A-Tier: 80-100
- B-Tier: 65-79
- C-Tier: 50-64
- D-Tier: Below 50
