# Matching Weight Customization

## Default Weights

| Dimension | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Skills Match | 30% |
| Experience Alignment | 25% |
| Requirements Fit | 25% |
| Growth & Culture | 10% |
| Practical Factors | 10% |

## Career Stage Adjustments

- **Entry Level (0-2 yrs):** Skills 20%, Experience 15%, Requirements 25%, Growth 25%, Practical 15%
- **Mid-Level (3-7 yrs):** Use defaults
- **Senior (8-15 yrs):** Skills 25%, Experience 30%, Requirements 25%, Growth 10%, Practical 10%
- **Executive (15+ yrs):** Skills 15%, Experience 35%, Requirements 25%, Growth 15%, Practical 10%
- **Freelance/Contract:** See `../shared-references/freelance-context.md`

## Industry Adjustments

- **Tech/Startups:** Skills +5%, Experience -5% (capability over tenure)
- **Finance/Consulting:** Experience +5%, Growth -5% (pedigree matters)
- **Government/Public Sector:** Requirements +10%, Growth -10% (strict qualifications)

## Scoring Thresholds

**Standard:** A 85-100, B 70-84, C 55-69, D <55
**Strict (competitive):** A 90-100, B 75-89, C 60-74, D <60
**Relaxed (broad search):** A 80-100, B 65-79, C 50-64, D <50
