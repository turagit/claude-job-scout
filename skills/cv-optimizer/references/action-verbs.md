# Action Verbs for CV Bullets

Start bullets with past-tense verbs (present for current role). Never repeat a verb within a role. Pair with quantifiable results. Choose verbs that convey **agency and ownership** — they trigger the authority heuristic.

## By Achievement Type

**Leadership & Influence:** Championed, Directed, Led, Orchestrated, Spearheaded, Transformed, Mentored, Steered, Mobilized, Appointed, Pioneered, Founded
**Delivery & Achievement:** Delivered, Exceeded, Secured, Solved, Surpassed, Completed, Shipped, Achieved, Attained, Won
**Technical & Engineering:** Architected, Automated, Built, Deployed, Engineered, Optimized, Scaled, Integrated, Migrated, Refactored, Implemented, Designed, Developed, Coded, Configured
**Analysis & Insight:** Analyzed, Benchmarked, Diagnosed, Forecasted, Quantified, Validated, Assessed, Evaluated, Identified, Investigated, Modeled, Mapped
**Growth & Revenue:** Boosted, Expanded, Generated, Launched, Doubled, Tripled, Grew, Accelerated, Increased, Captured, Monetized
**Process & Efficiency:** Eliminated, Modernized, Redesigned, Simplified, Standardized, Streamlined, Consolidated, Reduced, Cut, Halved, Shortened
**Communication & Collaboration:** Collaborated, Facilitated, Negotiated, Partnered, Presented, Advocated, Aligned, Influenced, Briefed, Coordinated

## By Psychological Impact

**Loss-prevention verbs** (trigger loss aversion): Prevented, Protected, Retained, Safeguarded, De-risked, Averted, Recovered, Rescued, Mitigated
**Exclusivity verbs** (trigger scarcity): Selected for, Invited to, Appointed as, Recruited to, Nominated for, Hand-picked
**Authority verbs** (trigger authority heuristic): Published, Certified, Advised, Consulted, Reviewed, Audited, Governed, Authored

## Verbs to Avoid

These signal passivity or lack of ownership — ATS may pass them but human reviewers discount them:

- Responsible for, Duties included, Helped with, Assisted in, Involved in, Participated in, Contributed to, Supported, Worked on, Tasked with, Handled

Replace with the specific action you took. "Responsible for managing deployments" → "Managed 200+ production deployments across 12 microservices with 99.97% uptime."
