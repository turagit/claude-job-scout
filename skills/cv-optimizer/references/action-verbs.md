# Action Verbs for CV Achievement Bullets

## Leadership & Management
Accelerated, Championed, Chaired, Consolidated, Coordinated, Delegated, Directed, Drove, Enabled, Established, Executed, Founded, Guided, Headed, Implemented, Initiated, Instituted, Led, Managed, Mentored, Mobilized, Orchestrated, Oversaw, Piloted, Pioneered, Prioritized, Spearheaded, Steered, Supervised, Transformed, Unified

## Achievement & Delivery
Accomplished, Achieved, Attained, Awarded, Completed, Delivered, Earned, Exceeded, Outperformed, Produced, Realized, Secured, Solved, Succeeded, Surpassed, Won

## Technical & Engineering
Architected, Automated, Built, Coded, Configured, Debugged, Deployed, Designed, Developed, Engineered, Integrated, Migrated, Modularized, Optimized, Programmed, Provisioned, Refactored, Scaled, Shipped, Streamlined, Tested

## Analysis & Research
Analyzed, Assessed, Audited, Benchmarked, Calculated, Compared, Diagnosed, Discovered, Evaluated, Examined, Forecasted, Identified, Interpreted, Investigated, Mapped, Measured, Modeled, Quantified, Researched, Surveyed, Tracked, Validated

## Growth & Revenue
Acquired, Boosted, Captured, Converted, Doubled, Expanded, Generated, Grew, Increased, Launched, Maximized, Monetized, Multiplied, Raised, Recovered, Scaled, Tripled, Upsold

## Process & Operations
Centralized, Eliminated, Improved, Modernized, Overhauled, Redesigned, Reduced, Reorganized, Restructured, Revamped, Simplified, Standardized, Systematized, Upgraded

## Communication & Influence
Advised, Advocated, Briefed, Collaborated, Communicated, Consulted, Convinced, Educated, Facilitated, Influenced, Negotiated, Partnered, Persuaded, Presented, Promoted, Published, Trained

## Usage Tips
- Always start bullets with a past-tense action verb (present tense only for current role)
- Never repeat the same verb within a single role's bullets
- Choose verbs that match the achievement scale: "Spearheaded" for leading large initiatives, "Contributed" for team efforts
- Pair action verbs with quantifiable results: "Reduced deployment time by 60%"
