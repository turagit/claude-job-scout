# Recruiter Response Templates

## Initial Response Templates

### Enthusiastic Interest (Hot Lead)
>
> Hi [Name], thank you so much for reaching out! The [Role] at [Company] sounds like a fantastic opportunity, and it aligns really well with my background in [relevant expertise].
>
> I'd love to learn more about the role and the team. A few questions:
>
> - What does the compensation range look like for this position?
> - Is this a remote/hybrid/on-site role?
> - Could you share more about the team I'd be working with?
>
> I'm happy to jump on a quick call this week if that works for you. Looking forward to hearing more!

### Cautious Interest (Warm Lead)
>
> Hi [Name], thanks for thinking of me — I appreciate you reaching out!
>
> I'd be open to learning more about what you have in mind. Could you share the job description and some details about the role? Specifically, I'd love to know about the compensation range and work arrangement before we go further.
>
> Looking forward to your reply!

### Polite Decline (Cold Lead)
>
> Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out! I appreciate you considering me for this opportunity.
>
> After looking into it, this particular role isn't quite the right fit for me at the moment. However, I'd be happy to stay connected — feel free to reach out if something more aligned with [your target area] comes up in the future.
>
> Best of luck with the search!

## Follow-Up Templates

### After No Response (5-7 days)
>
> Hi [Name], just following up on my message from last week about the [Role] at [Company]. I'm still very interested in learning more if the position is still open.
>
> Happy to work around your schedule for a call. Looking forward to hearing from you!

### After Receiving Job Description
>
> Thanks for sharing the details, [Name]! I've reviewed the job description and it looks like a strong match with my experience in [specific area].
>
> I have a couple of follow-up questions:
>
> - [Specific question about the role]
> - [Question about interview process/timeline]
>
> I'd love to move forward with the conversation!

### After Phone Screen — Expressing Continued Interest
>
> Hi [Name], thank you for taking the time to chat today! I really enjoyed our conversation about [specific topic discussed].
>
> The role sounds even more exciting after learning about [detail from conversation]. I'm enthusiastic about the opportunity and look forward to the next steps.
>
> Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything from my end!

## Handling Specific Scenarios

### Recruiter Asks for Salary Expectations
>
> I'm flexible and open to discussing compensation as we learn more about the role. Could you share the budgeted range for this position? That would help us make sure we're aligned early on.

### Recruiter Asks for Availability Immediately
>
> I appreciate the urgency! I'm currently [employed/on notice]. I could potentially start within [timeframe]. When would you need someone to begin?

### Agency Recruiter — Need More Details
>
> Thanks for reaching out! Before we discuss further, could you share:
>
> - The company name (I want to avoid any conflicts with other processes)
> - The full job description
> - The compensation range
>
> This helps me give you a more informed response. Thanks!

### Multiple Recruiters for Same Company
>
> Thanks for reaching out about [Company]! I wanted to let you know that I'm already in conversation with another recruiter about a role there. I want to be transparent to respect everyone's time.
>
> If this is a different position, I'd love to hear about it!

## Freelance / Contract Templates

### Freelance Hot Lead — Express Interest + Rate Question
>
> Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out! The [Role] at [Company/Client] looks like a great fit for my experience in [relevant area].
>
> Before we go further, a few quick questions:
>
> - What's the **day rate range** for this contract?
> - What's the **expected duration** (and is there extension potential)?
> - Is this **outside or inside IR35**?
> - Is this **fully remote**?
>
> I'm currently available from [date]. Happy to have a quick call to discuss!

### Freelance Warm Lead — Qualification First
>
> Hi [Name], thanks for thinking of me!
>
> I'd be interested to hear more. Could you share a few details before we dive in?
>
> - The **day rate or rate range**
> - **Contract duration** and any extension potential
> - **IR35 status** (inside/outside)
> - **Remote/on-site arrangement**
> - The **end client** (if you're able to disclose)
>
> This helps me give you a quick yes/no and saves us both time. Thanks!

### Freelance — Rate Negotiation
>
> Thanks for sharing the details, [Name]. The role sounds interesting, but the rate of [offered rate] is below my current range.
>
> Based on my experience in [area] and the market rate for this type of work, I'd be looking at [target rate]/day. Is there any flexibility on the budget?
>
> Happy to discuss further if so!

### Freelance — Availability Check
>
> Thanks for reaching out! I'm currently on a contract until [date], so my earliest availability would be [date]. Would that timeline work?
>
> If so, I'd love to hear more about the role and rate.
