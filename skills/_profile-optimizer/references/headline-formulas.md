# LinkedIn Headline Formulas

## Patterns

1. **Role + Specialty + Value:** `[Role] | [Specialty] | [Value Statement]`
2. **Role + Skills + Industry:** `[Role] specializing in [Skill1] & [Skill2] for [Industry]`
3. **Role + Keyword Stack:** `[Role] | [Keyword] | [Keyword] | [Keyword]`
4. **Impact-Led:** `[Achievement] | [Role] at [Company Type]`
5. **Career Transition:** `[Target Role] | Background in [Previous] | [Bridge Skill]`

## Guidelines
- Use all 220 characters; first 60 are critical (mobile preview)
- Include 2-3 searchable keywords
- Update when changing job search targets
- Avoid: "Open to Work" as headline (use badge), buzzwords without substance, company-only headlines
